— Who We Are

About Us ✍🏻

Mount Manbri & Mount Bypass is a gaming community that provides fun and challenging obby-based gameplay for all players.

We support voice chat, allowing more realistic and interactive communication between players. In Mount Bypass, there is a dedicated area where players can relax, socialize, and get to know each other — making our community not just a place to play, but also a place to build friendships.

Our first map was established on August 19, 2025, and was developed solo with passion and dedication. Since then we've grown into a thriving community.
